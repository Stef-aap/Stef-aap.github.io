Introduction



Devices can be simulated in three different ways:

  • virtual
  • digital
  • analog


Not all devices can operate in each mode.


device.kinds = [V,D,A]

Virtual Net

A virtual net always consists of 1 Virtual-Output and 1 Virtual-Input. The virtual "pin" can have many bits, e.g. 1 or 2 bytes. The virtual net should not be created by the user, but is automatically generated from the virtual JAL library.
