Gordon Williams improved some libraries

  

You might wonder, why I don't replace my own libraries with the improved ones of Gordon, well the reason is quiet simple, my installed base, build on my own routines (even if they are worse) is too large to switch.

VarDelay

is both smaller in compiled size (program and data) by 1/3 to 1/2 than the equivalent in jDelay. It is also *much* more accurate. Times are independent of clock speed or processor. It is a universal time delay.

  

FixDelay

provides an exact number of instruction cycles of delay.

  

BaseDelay

provides supports the other two.

Usage, etc are well documented in the headers.
