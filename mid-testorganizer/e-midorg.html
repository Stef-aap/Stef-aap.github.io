TestOrganizer (Dutch)
The TestOrganizer is a flexible and user friendly system for the registration, analysis and organization of all kinds of human and animal tests. The system is specially developped for use in the (para-)medical research. Both individual patient analysis / reporting as also group analysis (with export to all popular programms) is possible.
