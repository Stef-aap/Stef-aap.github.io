2-channel FysioFlex
Non-medical safe 2 channel Fysioflex, fits directly into RS232 or USB port of your PC (no power supply needed).
The input channels have a high gain amplifier and a bridge driver.
Input connections are equal to his large brother, so you can use the same cables and sensors.
Sample rates upto 1 kHz per channel.
Fixed sensitivity of 2,20,200 mVtt
