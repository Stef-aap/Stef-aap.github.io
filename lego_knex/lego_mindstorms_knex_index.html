Read My Comment

      

Building Robots with Lego Mindstorms, Mario Ferrari et. al.

The complete Mindstorms book for beginner and "professional".

The book contains nearly 600 pages and moreover the quality is as high as the number of pages. Following the links in each chapter, you'll get another 6000 pages of information for free.

The first weekend I had the book, I only catched a glimp of it. My son of 8 (not capable of reading any English) spent almost the whole weekend browsing in the book, he couldn't decide what to build first. Finally he decided to build the pinball machine. We are still improving  and playing with this machine, which gave us already a couple of weeks of pleasure.

Although the book doesn't contain any extended building instructions, it's full of ideas with enough explanation to get started and even more important .... to get finished. Designing good mechanical constructions with Lego Technics is quiet difficult. This books explains all the fundamentals and gives you good hints to build in a "modular" way. Both principle and practical examples of autonomous robots and controled machines are extensively described. The software snippets are largely in NQC, the most popular advanced programming language for the RCX`, using the standard firmware.

And what about music, pneumatics, flight simulator, games and much more, it's all there.

The only thing I missed was AI (Artificial Inteligence), but maybe that's to far away for the RCX.
