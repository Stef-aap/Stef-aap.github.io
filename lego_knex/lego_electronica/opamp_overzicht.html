I think it's not very important which amplifiers you choose, but you have to choose. Therfore I made a list, in which availability in the Netherlands and price are the most important parameters. For further information see selected parameters.
In the Netherlands, the most commonly used opamps in the hobby-segment at this moment are LM324, TL084, TL074 and TLC272 (derived from Elektuur)..

For Lego sensors, I think,  the following parameter values are preferable

  • available in packages of 2 and 4
  • low power
  • output swing 0 ... Vcc-2V
  • input common mode swing close to 0 ..
  • high input impedance
Values are worst case, links in column Type links directly to the datasheets, in column producer to an overview of relevant opamps.
