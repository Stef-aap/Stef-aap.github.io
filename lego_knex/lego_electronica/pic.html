work in progress

Inspired by some applications of the 16F84 PIC, we came up with the idea to make a IR-module, which is capable of doing all the InfraRed tasks we could imagine with the RCX.

1. Remote Control for the RCX (like the one in the ultimate accessory kit)

2. RCX responder, a stand alone device capable of detecting RCX-messages and responding with another RCX-message (therefor the device should be programmable).

3. Standard IR-tower (a lot of people has problems with the standard IR-tower), in such a way that the old IR tower can be put in parallel for extra IR transmitting power.

4. Shooting target, with 2 or more (visible?) light diodes produce a beep when the target is hit

5. Connected as an sensor to the RCX, so on the one hand the RCX can control audio and video systems and on the other hand the RCX can react on standard remote control units.

6. Infrared repeater, to enlarge the transmission region

7. Infrared buoy, which sometimes sends IR signals, so the RCX must try to locate the buoy
