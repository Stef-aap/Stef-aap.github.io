Sony TV-IR 

Measurements were done with the UIRT from Ruud van Gessel.

I couldn't get the DLL from Ruud van Gessel working with Delphi-5. I translated the header files both with Pascal and Cdecl declarations. Naming of the functions in the DLL wasn't possible, probably due to the name-mangling from the C-compiler. So I called the functions by index, which resulted in function 1: access violation, and the other functions (2 and 3) didn't do much.

So I wrote his DLL code in plain Delphi, using CPDRV commport driver.
And this seems to work, at least for the slow UIR mode and with a little imagination also for the fast mode.

In the measurements below, chan and volume could be exchanged, I'm not sure anymore.

Below the UIR mode, both in slow and fast RS232-mode.
The results of both are quiet stable, but I don't understand the coding scheme, and why is slow and fast serial different.
It seems that in the fast RS232 mode, the msb is always 1 or is it coincidence ?

Commands 

$20 + CS ,  sets the UIRT in UIR, but returns $EC
$21 + CS ,  sets the UIRT in RAW, but returns $EC
$22 + CS ,  sets the UIRT in STRUCT, but returns $EC
$23 + CS ,  (version), returns  $E0 $E0 $FF
